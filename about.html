about us.

Welcome to soo‐thé! We are a tea house specializing in a broad array of true and herbal teas, as well as a variety of accompanying side dishes. With an ambient sound system, comfy chairs, board games, and a well‐stocked bookcase, we provide an atmosphere where anyone can stop to unwind, tea-enthusiast or otherwise.
